About

This is a scratchpad for building custom formats for Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets.

It was made by your friends at Gridmaster.

If you like it, we think you will also like our animated spreadsheet tips, or our addin for learning Excel.

Special thanks to the SheetJS SSF project, which powers the formatting engine.

Issues

The Custom Formats Builder is in beta. Here are some things it's currently missing:

  • Support for format colors
  • Support for format conditions
  • Support for mixed or unreduced fractions
  • Support for language codes (LCIDs)
  • Alignment symbols (like ? and *) don't affect the alignment of the formatted cell.

If you notice any other issues or missing features, please report them here.
